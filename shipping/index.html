Shipping Policy

Every RUDOHAGE piece is carefully handcrafted, inspected, and packaged before dispatch. Please review our shipping guidelines below.

1. Order Processing

All orders are processed after payment confirmation. Since RUDOHAGE products are handcrafted, processing times may vary depending on product availability and order volume.

  • Standard Processing Time: 1–3 Business Days
  • Custom Orders: May require additional time
  • Orders are not processed on Sundays or public holidays

2. Delivery Timeline

Delivery times vary based on your location and courier partner availability.

  • Metro Cities: 3–7 Business Days
  • Other Locations: 5–10 Business Days
  • Remote Areas: Additional delivery time may apply

3. Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, tracking information will be shared through the contact details provided during checkout whenever available.

4. Cash On Delivery Orders

Cash On Delivery orders require a mandatory ₹50 Security Charge paid online before order confirmation.

  • Helps prevent fraudulent orders.
  • COD orders are processed only after security payment.
  • The remaining order amount is paid upon delivery.

5. Failed Delivery Attempts

  • Please ensure your address and phone number are accurate.
  • Repeated failed delivery attempts may result in order cancellation.
  • Additional shipping charges may apply for re-dispatch requests.

6. Shipping Delays

While we strive to deliver within estimated timelines, delays caused by courier services, weather conditions, public holidays, or unforeseen circumstances are outside our direct control.

7. International Shipping

Currently, RUDOHAGE primarily serves customers within India. International shipping availability may be introduced in the future.

8. Damaged Packages

If your package arrives visibly damaged, please take photographs immediately and contact us within 48 hours of delivery for assistance.
