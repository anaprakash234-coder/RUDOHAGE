Meet the Artisan

Every RUDOHAGE piece is shaped by hand, not machine. Our artisan blends traditional woodcraft with a modern eye — measuring, carving, and finishing each clock alone, start to finish, so no two pieces ever turn out exactly the same.

Frequently Asked

How long does shipping take?

Orders are dispatched within 3-5 business days and shipped free across India with tracking provided.

Is every piece really one of a kind?

Yes. Each item is handcrafted individually and never reproduced, so natural variations in grain and finish are part of its character.

Can I return or exchange my order?

Reach out within 48 hours of delivery if there's a genuine issue and our team will help sort it out.

How do I care for my piece?

Keep it away from direct sunlight and moisture, and wipe gently with a soft dry cloth to preserve the finish.
