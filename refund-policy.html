Refund Policy

Every RUDOHAGE piece is handcrafted individually for its owner. Please review our refund guidelines before placing an order.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refund requests are accepted only if the product arrives damaged, defective, or significantly different from the item ordered. Requests must be submitted within 48 hours of delivery.

2. Damaged Products

  • Customers must provide clear photos of the damaged item.
  • Images should be submitted within 48 hours of delivery.
  • After verification, a replacement or refund may be issued.
  • RUDOHAGE reserves the right to approve or reject claims after inspection.

3. Non-Refundable Orders

  • Custom-made or personalized products.
  • Products damaged due to misuse.
  • Orders delivered successfully as described.
  • Change of mind after purchase.

4. Cash On Delivery Security Charge

A mandatory ₹50 Security Charge is collected before confirming Cash On Delivery orders.

  • The charge helps prevent fraudulent orders.
  • The charge is non-refundable once the order is confirmed.
  • Cancelled orders after confirmation may not qualify for refund of the security charge.

5. Refund Processing Time

  • Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.
  • Bank processing times may vary.
  • Refunds are issued through the original payment method whenever possible.

6. Order Cancellation

Orders may only be cancelled before processing begins. Once production, packaging, or shipment starts, cancellation requests cannot be guaranteed.
