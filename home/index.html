About Our Brand

RUDOHAGE was founded by Anand Prakash with a single belief — true luxury lies in precision. Every piece is handcrafted, layer by layer, using premium materials and time-honored techniques. We do not mass produce. We create legacy pieces designed to age beautifully.

Our workshop in India is a sanctuary of craftsmanship, where each creation undergoes a meticulous process — from careful material selection to the final finishing touches. We work with premium woods, leathers, and metals, combining traditional methods with contemporary design. Every RUDOHAGE piece is a collector's item, designed to be passed down through generations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you use?

We use premium hand-carved wood, full-grain leather, and high-quality metals sourced from sustainable suppliers in India.

How long does shipping take?

We offer free shipping across India. Delivery typically takes 5-7 business days. International shipping is available on request.

Are these products really handcrafted?

Yes! Every piece is 100% handcrafted by our master artisans. No mass production or automation is used in our process.

Can I request a custom order?

Absolutely! We love creating custom pieces. Contact us directly with your requirements and we'll bring your vision to life.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy on unused items in original condition. Please refer to our Refund Policy page for details.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship internationally. Please contact us with your location and we'll provide shipping rates and estimated delivery times.
